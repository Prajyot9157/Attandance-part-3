Google Sheets Integrated Solution

Setup Instructions

Follow these steps to connect this form to your Google Sheet:

Important: This form must be hosted on a web server (like GitHub Pages) to work with FormSubmit.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Go to sheets.google.com and create a new spreadsheet
  2. Name the first row headers: Timestamp, RollNumber, Name, Subject, Date
  3. Click the "Share" button and set to "Anyone with the link can edit"
  4. Copy your spreadsheet URL and paste it in the form below
  5. Click "Save Configuration" to connect your sheet

FormSubmit Configuration

For FormSubmit to save data to your Google Sheet, you need to:

  1. Share your Google Sheet with formsubmit.co@gmail.com as an editor
  2. Rename your sheet to "FormSubmit Responses"
  3. Wait a few minutes after submission for the data to appear

Setup Complete!

Your attendance system is now connected to Google Sheets.

  • Use Filters to view attendance by date or subject

Troubleshooting: If data isn't appearing in your sheet:
  1. Make sure you shared the sheet with formsubmit.co@gmail.com
  2. Check that the sheet is named "FormSubmit Responses"
  3. Wait a few minutes after form submission
